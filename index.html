About

A two-decade career arc — from engineering rigor to enterprise agility to AI-first leadership.

Over 20 years, I have navigated and led organizations through every major inflection point in software delivery. I began my career in CMMI-governed environments — where process discipline, compliance audits, and engineering rigor defined success. As the industry evolved, I became an early champion of Agile adoption, guiding teams through the transition from Waterfall to Scrum, SAFe, Kanban, and Spotify-inspired operating models — transforming not just processes, but mindsets and cultures across globally distributed enterprises.

Today, I operate at the frontier of AI-first delivery — designing AI-augmented execution frameworks, establishing AI governance models, enabling teams with AI-powered decision-making tools, and building intelligent dashboards that drive predictability at portfolio scale. I don't just adopt what's next — I architect the path to get there.

As a trusted partner to C-level leaders, I bring a rare combination: the execution discipline of PMO governance, the adaptive leadership of Agile transformation, and the strategic vision of AI enablement. Whether standing up an ART, redesigning delivery controls, or launching an enterprise AI Lab — I lead with clarity, conviction, and measurable outcomes.

Beyond delivery, I am deeply invested in growing people — conducting org-wide trainings, serving on hiring panels to build high-caliber teams, and mentoring the next generation through University Academy Programs (UAP) at colleges, bridging the gap between industry and academia.
